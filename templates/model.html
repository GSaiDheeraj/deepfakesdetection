The Model

We have developed a custom Convolutional Net Architecture for detecting the deep fake Images. We have trained the model with 1,00,000 (1 lakh) Images and validated and tested on 40,000 Images. We have used Cut-Out Regularization for generalizing the model on new images. As a result we achieved an Accuracy of 97.73% on train data and 97.825% on validation data(20,000 Images) and 96.73% accuracy on testing data(20,000 Images) with precision of 99.48% and sensitivity of 93.94%.
The final Dense layer uses Softmax activation function, it returns a probablity of the sample to be a Fake or Real. For that reason, this classifer is only useful to differentiate Real Images and Fake Images generated by GAN's.
